1. Eligibility

  • The Refer and Earn program is open to all registered participants of the Warzone Tournament.
  • A user must complete their registration before receiving a referral code.

2. Referral Rewards

  • Registered users can share their referral code with friends and other potential participants.
  • For every successful referral, the referrer will receive 10% of the referred user’s registration fee.
  • A referral is considered successful only when the referred user completes the registration and payment process.

3. Referral Code Usage

  • The referral code of every registered user is their registered mobile number.
  • The referred participant must enter the referral code during registration forms for the referrer to earn rewards.
  • Referral rewards will not be applicable if the code is not entered correctly at the time of registration.

4. Payout and Reward Distribution

  • Referral rewards will be credited to the referrer after the tournament registration period ends.
  • Rewards may be distributed via online payment methods or tournament credits, as per the organizer’s discretion.
  • Any fraudulent activity or misuse of referral codes may result in disqualification and forfeiture of earnings.

5. General Terms & Conditions

  • The decision of the tournament organizers regarding any disputes related to referrals shall be final and binding.
  • Any violation of tournament rules or unethical behavior may result in disqualification from the program and the tournament.
